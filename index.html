Julián Kancepolski
 
Julián Kancepolski es un compositor nacido en Buenos Aires, en 2001 se radicó en España. Ha editado tres discos: Portbou (2007), Amateur (2019) y Solarium (2020). Además, crea e interpreta música para cine, televisión, danza, teatro y medios digitales.

Julián Kancepolski is a composer born in Buenos Aires who settled in Spain in 2001. He has released three albums: Portbou (2007), Amateur (2019), and Solarium (2020). He also creates, arranges, and performs music for film, television, theatre, dance, and other media.
